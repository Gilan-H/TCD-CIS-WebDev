The National Puzzlers League

The National Puzzlers League has done many things past their creation of the famous english word pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis, they are actually the worlds oldest puzzlers organization, founded in 1883. They also have a monthly publication of the ENIGMA and have produced a number of full length puzzle books. The majortiy of their puzzles are classified as "flats" a puzzle that includes wordplay. Each ENIGMA published is filled to the brim with puzzles For their most dedicated readers they have a yearly convention at varying locations, this year in Minneapolis. The National Puzzlers League has close to 900 paying members.
